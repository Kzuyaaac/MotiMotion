Moti Motion - A Social Simulation Game

Platform: PC

Engine: Unity

Developer:

  • Diang A R (Game Design & Art)
  • Shakira (Art)
  • Zahir Raihan Hakim (Programming)

Published: January 28, 2024

Description:

Moti Motion is a social simulation game where your task is to help Moti blend in with the crowd by matching facial expressions with the mood of the audience. Each stage challenges you to match the crowd's emotions accurately. Can you stay in the show by maintaining the right facial expressions?

Key Features:

  • Emotion Matching Mechanic: Choose the right expression to match the audience.
  • Simple and Fun Gameplay: Click to select the correct emotion—time your choices carefully!
  • Challenging Stages: Each level requires you to match 3-4 emotions to win.
  • Limited Attempts: Make 3 wrong choices, and you're out of the show!
  • Cute Pixel Art: Enjoy a charming pixel art style that makes the game visually appealing and fun.
  • Short Expression Timers: Each emotion takes 5 seconds to switch—stay alert!

Development Story:

Moti Motion was developed during the Global Game Jam (GGJ) 2024, with the theme "Make Me Laugh." Originally planned as a larger team of 8 members, our team of just 3 successfully completed the game using Unity for PC in 3 days. It was a fun and challenging project!

Gameplay Instructions:

Use the on-screen emoji buttons to match the audience's expressions. Each stage requires you to match 3-4 expressions correctly. You have 3 attempts—fail three times, and you'll lose the game. Keep an eye on the timer: expressions take 5 seconds to change.

Game Keybinds:

Action Key Description
Choose Expression Left Mouse Click Click to select the correct emotion that matches the crowd
